ORANGE is a Vietnamese Pop R&B singer-songwriter whose debut song was one of the biggest hits in Vpop history. Her song “Nguoi La Oi” helped her win Best New Asian Artist at MAMA 2018. She also represented Vietnam to compete in 2019 Hong Kong Asian Music Festival and became the champion.

In 2024, ORANGE released her self-written debut album “CAM’ON”, which was a remarkable milestone in her career. According to ORANGE, her songs are a journal of her growth as a woman and a musician.
