JSOL joined DreamS Entertainment to realize his dream of becoming a singer. JSOL released its first product in 2017, the song ‘Lặng’, this song was designed by Rhymastic for JSOL's voice. 
Subsequent music products of JSOL were born in turn with the refinement of vocals and images, which can be mentioned as: ‘Giá Như Em Nhìn Lại’, ‘Hết Yêu Thật Sao’, 'Còn Yêu Sao Nói Không Hợp’, ‘Hiếm Có Khó Tìm’, ‘Sài Gòn Hôm Nay Mưa’. 
JSOL has made a lot of impressions in The Heroes 2021 competition.
