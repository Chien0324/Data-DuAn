Captain Boy (full name Hoàng Đức Duy, born in 2003, and raised in Hòa Bình City) is a dynamic Gen Z rapper, singer, and songwriter who first made waves in "Rap Việt Season 3" program. Known for his versatile talents, he impressed audiences with his rap, vocal, and songwriting abilities. A former valedictorian of Thăng Long University’s Vocal Music program, he has continued to evolve, captivating fans on "Anh Trai Say Hi 2024" program. His standout tracks include Rolling Down, Quên Dần Quên, Mong Em Sẽ Cố Quên, Kim Tự Tháp, and Ừ Thì Chia Tay.
