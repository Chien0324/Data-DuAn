- Song Luan has the birth name is Nguyen Truong Sinh, was born on September 17, 1991 in Dong Nai. He initially worked as a singer and joined the Music Box Entertainment of singer Thanh Thao. After the second participating in the Vietnamese Voice contest, he signed a contract with 6th Sense Entertainment in 2015. - In 2016, he had his first role in the movie Iron Box. In 2018, he played the main role of Lat Mat: Ba Chang Khuyet - a film of Ly Hai director. - He won the "Best Actor" award in the Vietnam region at the 2019 Asian Creative Academy Awards as Duy Kien in the Vietnamese TV series Descendants of the Sun. - In 2021, he starred in the famous film - Cay Tao No Hoa
